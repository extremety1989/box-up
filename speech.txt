Welcome to the tutorial. In this tutorial, you will learn how to ajust floor position, do basic punches and move around the room.

Let's start with adjusting the floor position.
Click and Hold the "Adjust floor position" button.

Position the white plane right to the floor and release the button.

Great! If you are ready, Press any of A,B,X,Y buttons to continue with the tutorial.

Check your boxing gloves. You need to hit the yellow target with the yellow glove and the black target with the black glove. 
Now, let's do simple left-right punches.

Ok, let's do now right, left.

Next, let's do uppercuts, left and right, followed by hook punches, left and right.

Now let's do one squat, then sky punches, followed by hammer punches, alternating left and right. 
You can use both hands on sky and hammer punches if needed.

Well done, now you know how to play. You can adjust the difficulty to easy, medium, or hard. 
To play, press the thumbstick button on the left or right controller. You must be on the green spot to play or stop the game. 
You can also teleport to other yellow spots to admire the room. Have fun!
